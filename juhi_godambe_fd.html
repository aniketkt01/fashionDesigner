JUHI GODAMBE

Juhi Godambe is an Indian Fashion Blogger for Luxury fashion and lifestyle. She’s born on May 12, 1994. She started initially with a high street fashion brand Arabella after completing her college at the London School of trends. Although she’s born and brought up in Mumbai.In Josh talks, Juhi mentioned that she was an introverted kid who would get intimidated as she saw people around her. It was after the 10th that she realized she was passionate about fashion and would like to take that further as her career.
