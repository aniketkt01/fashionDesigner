Diipa Khosla

Diipa Khosla is a fashion and beauty influencer & cofounder of her foundation Post For Change. When she was 17 years old, she moved to the Netherlands for studies. After which she did an internship at IMA Influencer Agency in Amsterdam where she got information about social media and she was introduced to the world of social media.She started her career in 2015. She started her Instagram account in May 2015 and now she has 1.2 million followers. She has now become an Instagram star. She was named the Elle India’s influencer of the year in 2019.

  • Influencer of the Year Award.
